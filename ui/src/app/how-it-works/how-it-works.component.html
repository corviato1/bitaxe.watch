How It Works

When you provide an email and Bitcoin address, the system will frequently check (roughly every 20 minutes) to see if there have been any transactions related to the Bitcoin address. If there have been, it will send you an email alert that includes the new balance of the address.

I developed this service because I wanted to be notified when payments came into or out of my Bitcoin addresses, without signing up or paying for a service. I also appreciate services that collect minimal personal information, so I tried to provide that here as well. I also wanted to publish my own Bitcoin-related project, and this was a good opportunity to do so. I hope you find it useful!
